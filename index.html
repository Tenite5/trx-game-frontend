About Voronoi Hex

  • Stake: 10 TRX per game
  • Winner takes: 19.5 TRX (2.5% platform fee)
  • Goal: Connect your assigned sides
  • Red Player: Connect top to bottom
  • Blue Player: Connect left to right
  • New players: Start with 100 TRX bonus!
